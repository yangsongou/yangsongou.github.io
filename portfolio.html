Introduction

The product of my team is an mobile application of using autonomous shopping cart at supermarket. It aims to help customers find out items and shop efficiently. Users can prepare a shopping list on the application before they go to the supermarket. Once users scan the QR code on the autonomous shopping cart and connect with it, the connected autonomous shopping cart will follow users until they finish shopping. Then users can conduct the shopping list through AR navigation or add any items they want but not on the shopping list. Also, the application provides self-checkout function for users to achieve an efficient shopping experience.

Design process overview

For the design process, we go background research, user research, ideation, wireframe sketches, paper prototype, user evaluation of paper prototype, digital prototype, user evaluation of digital prototype and final refinement in order. Initially, we had two ideas based on one core concept of finding items and we created two paper prototypes for them, in order to save the better one for further development after user evaluation. Also, during the design process, we did iteration at every stage and made improvements based on users feedback to achieve the best solution.

Reflection

Users are the most important factor in our design process. From user research, we defined the problem that customers waste time on finding items at supermarket, then explore potential solutions for the problem. Also, we found better solution through user evaluations and iterated the prototype based on user feedback. So, gathering more and more user feedback during design process is a good way to improve our design.
